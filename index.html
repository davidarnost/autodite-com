About

Meet the Founder

David Arnost

I started Autodite because I’ve watched businesses lose enormous amounts of time to tasks that should never need a person to do them.

I spent over five years in Sales and Marketing at Nissan, Genesis, and Hyundai, working across customer services, sales operations, strategy, and product marketing. The systems were built for a different era. The work was often data-heavy: pulling from databases, building reports by hand, and assembling presentations that would be out of date before they were finished.

In Sales Operations, one recurring job refused to get easier. Every quarter, I built a full dealer review deck from scratch - pulling data, formatting tables, assembling it slide by slide. Fifteen hours of work, every single time. So I taught myself to automate it. The same job took ten minutes.

That wasn’t the end of it. A monthly report that had taken five hours started running in thirty minutes, and landed eleven days earlier than it used to. I’d gone from dreading certain weeks to barely noticing them.

The tools available today make what I figured out back then dramatically faster, more powerful, and accessible to any business without a dedicated technical team. I work exclusively with tools built to be understood by business people, not developers. I came to this as a business person myself, and that’s exactly the position I work from with every client: you do not need a technical background, and you don’t need to know where your time is going. Finding that out is the first thing we do together.

I’m not a financial adviser, and I won’t pretend to be. My job is your time and your systems, not the advice. But advice firms are full of exactly the kind of slow, manual, data-heavy work I’ve spent years turning into something that runs in minutes.

My approach is practical and jargon-free. I’m not here to impress you with technical language. I’m here to find you time.
